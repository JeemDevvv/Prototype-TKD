Coach Rene

Head Coach

Coach Rene is the Head Coach of ARISE Taekwondo Team, bringing years of experience and dedication to training athletes. Under his leadership, the team has achieved numerous championships and continues to excel in both Poomsae and Kyorugi disciplines. His commitment to excellence and passion for Taekwondo inspires all team members to reach their full potential.

Coach Aldrin

Kyorugi Coach

Coach Aldrin specializes in Kyorugi (sparring) training, focusing on competitive techniques and strategic combat skills. As a former competitive athlete, he brings practical experience and advanced training methods to help athletes excel in tournaments. His expertise in sparring tactics and physical conditioning has produced many successful competitors.

Coach Vince

Poomsae Coach

Coach Vince is the Poomsae (forms) specialist, dedicated to perfecting the art and precision of Taekwondo forms. His attention to detail and technical expertise helps athletes master the intricate movements and patterns required for Poomsae competitions. Under his guidance, team members develop grace, precision, and artistic expression in their forms.

ARISE TAEKWONDO TEAM

The Arise Taekwondo Team was born and developed through the strong foundation and support of EARIST. From its humble beginnings, the team has grown into a dedicated community that upholds the values of discipline, respect, and perseverance. What started as a school-based initiative has evolved into a thriving organization that now runs multiple training schools and continues to build up aspiring athletes into skilled and confident players. Guided by passion and excellence, the Arise Taekwondo Team stands as a testament to teamwork, growth, and the enduring spirit of martial arts.
